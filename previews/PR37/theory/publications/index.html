Publications

A curated reading list, organized by the role each work plays in SigmaTau's foundation. For the alphabetical reference index, see the Bibliography.

Foundational

  • Riley & Howe 2008 — Handbook of Frequency Stability Analysis, NIST Special Publication 1065. The canonical reference for the Allan / Modified-Allan / Total / Hadamard family definitions, bias factors, and EDF approximations that SigmaTau's Stab submodule reproduces.
  • IEEE 1139-2022 — Standard Definitions of Physical Quantities for Fundamental Frequency and Time Metrology. The current normative source for stability-statistic notation, units, and reporting conventions.
